# Device Compatibility

The WPE3V2 Safety Beam works with the following automatic openers, manufactured by ATA, B\&D, Garador and Dominator:

* SmartPro
* Smart
* Secure
* PanelMax
* Tempo
* Syncro
* Genius HD
* Genius
* Precision
